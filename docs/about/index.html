About

Hey! I'm Arnav, a full-stack engineer and grad student at SJSU, pursuing a Master's in Computer Science. I spent two years at PayPal building GenAI-powered fraud detection systems, and before that, worked on automating infrastructure provisioning for ETL pipelines at Hevo Data. I have also built and shipped multiple cross-platform apps and websites as a founding engineer for a few startups during my undergrad.

Recently, I have been obsessed with hackathons, something about ideating and building to compete in 24 hours is addictive. It almost models the competition that happens on a large scale in tech. My recent wins include Cal Hacks 12.0, Solana Renaissance, HackNight at GitHub, HackwithBay at Microsoft, Future of Agents Hackathon, Hack for Social Impact, Top 6 at Google's Gemini Hackathon for TED AI, the YCombinator hackathon, MLH Hackathon with Digital Ocean, and more!

Outside of coding, I believe some skills get unlocked when you walk the line between order and chaos. I've been dipping my feet into rock climbing, surfing, learning guitar (even performing solo on stage), skateboarding, and, honestly, anything that pushes me out of my comfort zone. These experiences have taught me resilience and adaptability in ways that sitting behind a screen never could. When I'm not building something or trying not to fall off a surfboard, you'll find me working on side projects or exploring new AI tools. Always down to chat about full-stack development, AI applications, or swap stories about learning something completely new!
